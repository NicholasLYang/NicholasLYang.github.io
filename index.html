About Me

Hi! My name is Nicholas. I am currently on a gap year in between Stuyvesant High School and NYU. I plan on majoring in Mathematics at NYU. I am experienced in Python, Ruby, Java and C++, along with frameworks such as Ruby on Rails and Flask. I write a blog called Horribly Underqualified . I try to focus on clean, readable code. While intelligent and tricky code may seem cool while writing it, it quickly becomes tedious or even painful to maintain. However, I'm far from an expert on this subject.
